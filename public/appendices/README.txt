Appendices PDFs
===============

The appendices PDFs are not currently available for direct download from the source websites.

To obtain the appendices:

1. Visit https://www.wikisubmission.org/appendices to read online
2. Visit https://masjidtucson.org/publications/ for publications
3. Contact the maintainers for PDF versions

The app has been configured to support local PDF hosting once the files are obtained.

To add PDFs:
- Place the complete appendices PDF as: appendices_complete.pdf
- Place individual appendix PDFs as: appendix_01.pdf, appendix_02.pdf, etc.
